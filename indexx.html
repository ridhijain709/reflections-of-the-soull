About This Collection

"रूह के अक्स" is a heartfelt journey through emotions—a collection of poetry and shayari woven with love, nostalgia, and self-discovery. Each piece captures a moment, a thought, or a fleeting feeling that lingers in the soul.

Inspired by the legendary Gulzar Sahab, this book is not just about rhymes; it's about emotions that breathe through every verse. Whether it's love, longing, or the unspoken whispers of the heart, these words aim to touch the deepest corners of your soul.

This book is dedicated to the dreamers, the wanderers, and those who find solace in the beauty of words. Welcome to a world where every word is a mirror, and every poem is a story untold.

Ridhi Jain

Biography

Born on March 7, 2006, in Muzaffarnagar, UP, Ridhi Jain's journey with poetry began at a young age, nurtured by her love for literature. She completed her senior secondary education in 2023, and her passion for writing only deepened over time.

Currently pursuing a BBA from MSU Saharanpur (SD College of Management Studies, Muzaffarnagar), poetry remains her constant companion, shaping her perspective on life and the world around her.

Inspired by the legendary Gulzar Sahab, Ridhi has learned that poetry is not just about rhymes but about the depth of emotions they carry. His words have been a guiding force, teaching her that true poetry comes from the heart and speaks to the soul.

"Each piece in this book is a reflection of my thoughts, experiences, and the world as I see it—a blend of love, nostalgia, self-discovery, and the unspoken emotions we all carry within us."

Behind the Book

The Creative Journey

"रूह के अक्स" is more than just a book—it's a piece of my soul, carefully crafted over years of introspection, emotions, and lived experiences. The journey of creating this collection began during my school days when I discovered my love for poetry through Gulzar Sahab's work.

Each poem in this book represents a different phase of my life—from the innocent love for my mother to the complexities of romantic relationships, from the joy of friendships to the philosophical musings about life itself.

"I wrote most of these poems late at night, when the world was quiet and my thoughts were loudest. Those silent hours became my sanctuary, where emotions transformed into words."
